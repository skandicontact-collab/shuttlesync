Destination Support & SKANDI Guides

Our local SKANDI guides are available around the clock, every day of the week. Connect directly through the SKANDI app, which you can download before your trip. Send messages to our guides and receive replies within 30 minutes—urgent inquiries are handled 24/7 via phone. At selected hotels and destinations, you can also meet our guides in person. The app provides guide service times and more just before departure.

SKANDI App – Your Personal Service Tool

  • Direct messaging with SKANDI guides – replies typically within 30 minutes
  • Transfer times to and from the airport
  • Important notifications (flight delays, schedule changes, etc.)
  • Destination and hotel information, inspiration, and tips
  • Book tours and activities easily from the app
  • Pre-order from the onboard and tax-free shop
How to access the SKANDI App:
1. Download the SKANDI App from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Activate your account by logging in with your SKANDI profile or booking number and the phone or email you used at booking.
3. Ensure you enable notifications for timely updates.

Our Service – Before, During & After Your Trip

  • Before your trip: Once you book, download and activate the SKANDI App to access all features. Feel free to ask our guides anything—from restaurant tips to sightseeing. Guides respond within 24 hours before travel.
  • Arrival: At most sun and city destinations, our English- and Scandinavian-speaking guides meet you at the airport. Use the app for transfer info or to locate your SKANDI transport. If you’ve booked private transfer, your driver will meet you directly.
  • During your stay: Reach our guides anytime via the SKANDI App for tips and support, or meet them during set hours at selected hotels. The app delivers key info and excursion offers throughout your holiday.
  • Excursions: Explore and book SKANDI’s excursions and events in the app or through your MySKANDI portal. You’ll get instant confirmation and ticketing for every experience.
Easy to reach us – whenever you need:
Support is available 24/7 via the app, chat, or phone (your number will appear in the app prior to departure).
Some booking types (e.g., hotel-only or cruise) may have limited app functionality. For cruises, please contact the cruise line directly using the information provided in your travel documents.
